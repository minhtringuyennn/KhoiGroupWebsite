We are excited to launch the MLR Lab blog! This space will be dedicated to sharing:

- **Research updates** — Summaries and insights from our latest papers
- **Tutorials** — How-to guides on tools and methods we use in our research
- **Lab news** — Conference presentations, new members, and milestones
- **Perspectives** — Opinions and observations on trends in ML and robotics

## What to Expect

Our goal is to make our research more accessible. Each post will aim to explain our work in a way that is understandable to a broad audience while still providing enough technical depth for practitioners in the field.

We will cover topics across our core research areas: 3D scene understanding, robot manipulation, embodied AI, and generative models for robotics.

## Get Involved

If you are interested in our work, feel free to reach out. We are always looking for talented researchers to join our team. Check out our [Opening](/opening) page for current positions.
