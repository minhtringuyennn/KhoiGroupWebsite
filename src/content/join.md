# Join Our Lab

We are always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join the MLR Lab!

## PhD Students

We accept 1–2 PhD students per year through the CMU School of Computer Science PhD program.
If you are interested in working with us, please apply to the [CMU SCS PhD program](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/academics/phd)
and mention Prof. Smith's name in your application.

**We are particularly looking for students with:**

- Strong background in machine learning and/or computer vision
- Experience with robotics or 3D vision (preferred but not required)
- Publications at top venues (NeurIPS, ICML, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ICRA, RSS)
- Solid programming skills in Python and PyTorch

Before applying, please read our recent papers to ensure your interests align with the lab's research directions.

## Postdoctoral Researchers

We occasionally have openings for postdoctoral researchers. If you are interested, please email
Prof. Smith with your CV, research statement, and 2–3 representative papers.

## MS Students at CMU

If you are a current CMU MS student interested in research, please email Prof. Smith with your CV
and a brief description of your research interests and relevant experience.

## Undergraduate Students

We welcome motivated undergraduate students for research projects. Please email Prof. Smith
with your CV and a description of your interests.

## Visiting Researchers

We occasionally host visiting students and researchers. Please email us with your CV and
the dates you are interested in visiting.

## How to Contact Us

Please email [mlrlab@cs.cmu.edu](mailto:mlrlab@cs.cmu.edu) with the subject line
**"[Position] — Your Name"** (e.g., "[PhD Inquiry] — Jane Doe").

Include the following in your email:

1. Your CV/resume
2. A brief (1-paragraph) description of your research interests and why you want to join our group
3. Links to any publications or projects
4. Your availability/start date

**Note:** Due to the high volume of emails, we may not be able to respond to all inquiries.
We appreciate your understanding.
